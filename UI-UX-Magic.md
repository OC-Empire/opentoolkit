## ✨ The "Soul" of the Interface

### A. Glassmorphism (Liquid Glass)
- **Inspiration**: Apple's revolutionary vision.
- **Tech**: SVG filters, WebGL, and GSAP animations.
- **Implementation**: Use `@marcosdemik/liquidglass` for refraction effects.
- **Visuals**: Translucent materials, dynamic light effects, and fluid animations.

### B. Micro-Interactions
- **Libraries**: [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) and [React Spring](https://www.react-spring.dev/).
- **Effects**:
  - **Tool Cards**: "Pop" effect on hover (scale 1.05).
  - **Starring**: Confetti explosion on click.
  - **Buttons**: Playful wobble when hovered.
- **Sound**: Subtle "pop" or "click" audio cues for key interactions.

### C. Color & Theme
- **Primary Palette**:
  - Background: `#0d1117` (Dark), `#f8fafc` (Light).
  - Accent: `#3b82f6` (Trust Blue).
- **Dark Mode**: CSS-based toggle (☀️/🌙) with a smooth transition.
